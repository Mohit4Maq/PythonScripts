Employee Handbook - Work from Home Policy

This document contains information about our work from home policies.

For detailed information about employee benefits and perks, including work from home policies, please visit:
https://resources.workable.com/employee-benefits-perks-policies

The work from home policy includes:
- One day per week allowed
- Manager approval required for more days
- Two days advance notice required
- Secure internet connection required
- Team check-ins required
- Emergency procedures for inclement weather
